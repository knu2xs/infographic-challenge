Infographics

Infographics are graphic visual representations of information, data, or knowledge intended to present information quickly and clearly. They can improve cognition by utilizing graphics to enhance the human visual system's ability to see patterns and trends. -Wikipedia

Infographics, like maps, if done correctly, provide a means, "to separate the signal from the noise (Dubrille, 2014). Combining geographic GIS analyses and visualization with infographics provdies a means to communicate the results of complex geographic analyses succicently.
